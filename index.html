Research Experience

Lesion Segmentation using Attention Double U-net

Class imbalance and loss of spatial data are two common problems when it comes to segmenting small lesion sections. We used focal tverksy loss to shift the emphasis to im- prove recall in the case of large class imbalance. We also introductioned attentioned Double U-net to pass relevant spatial data to the output layers.The model scores a 80.2% dice score which is 22.87% greater than widely used U-net.
